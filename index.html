SDA on Kyeyo Online Church

A virtual church uniting SDA members and others across the globe for worship and mutual support.

About Us

Our Story

SDA on Kyeyo Online Church was established in 2019 during the global COVID-19 pandemic. It provides a spiritual home for SDA members working abroad, particularly in the Arab world, as well as for non-SDA individuals seeking community and spiritual growth.

Our Mission

Our purpose is to facilitate worship, provide mutual support, and address challenges faced by members both spiritually and in their workplaces. We aim to foster a sense of belonging, offer guidance during difficult times, and empower members to thrive in their faith and professional lives.

Our Services

Spiritual Growth

Join our powerful weekly online worship services and Bible studies that are designed to uplift your spirit, deepen your connection with faith, and help you grow in your relationship with God. Experience transformation through prayer, reflection, and a supportive spiritual community.

Community Support

We provide a strong and compassionate network of individuals who share their life stories, offer encouragement, and provide the support needed to overcome life's challenges. Our community is here to guide you through difficult times, inspire you with powerful stories, and build a resilient support system for your well-being.

Workplace Empowerment

Break through the barriers of workplace stress and challenges with our expert advice and personalized mentorship. We equip you with the tools and strategies needed to succeed in your career, improve your work-life balance, and build resilience in a fast-paced and competitive environment.

Skill Development

Unlock your potential with our immersive workshops and training programs that focus on both personal and professional development. Whether you're looking to sharpen your leadership skills, master new technologies, or gain a competitive edge in your career, our expert-led sessions are designed to propel you toward success.

Financial Guidance

Gain the financial wisdom and strategies you need to secure your future. From effective saving techniques to investment planning and budgeting for members abroad, our experts provide tailored advice that empowers you to take control of your finances and build long-term financial stability.
